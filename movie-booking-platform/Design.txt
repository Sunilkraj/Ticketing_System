3. Detailed Use Case Descriptions
3.1. Book Tickets Use Case
Primary Actor: Customer
Preconditions: User is logged in, show exists, seats available
Main Flow:

Customer searches for shows by movie, city, and date

System displays available shows with timing and seat availability

Customer selects show and seats

System calculates price with applicable discounts

Customer confirms booking and makes payment

System confirms booking and sends confirmation

System updates seat inventory

Alternate Flows:

Seats not available: Suggest alternative shows/seats

Payment failed: Retry or cancel booking

Session timeout: Restart booking process

3.2. Manage Shows Use Case
Primary Actor: Theatre Partner
Preconditions: Partner is authenticated and authorized
Main Flow:

Partner logs into management portal

Partner selects theatre and screen

Partner creates new show with movie, timing, and pricing

System validates show details and availability

System creates show and updates inventory

Show becomes available for booking

Alternate Flows:

Timing conflict: Show overlapping prevention

Capacity exceeded: Validate against screen capacity

Movie not available: Check movie licensing
